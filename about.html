About Us

Saturny Pizza has been serving our community for over 30 years. We are a family-owned and operated business that is committed to providing high-quality food and excellent customer service.

Our History

We first opened our doors in 1989 and have been serving delicious pizza ever since. Our recipes have been passed down through generations of our family, and we are proud to continue the tradition of offering home-cooked pizza made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

Our Mission

At My Restaurant, our mission is to provide our customers with the best possible dining experience. We do this by creating delicious pizza, offering friendly service, and maintaining a welcoming atmosphere.

Our Values

  • Quality - We are committed to using only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients in all of our dishes.
  • Community - We believe in supporting our local community by sourcing our ingredients from local farmers and suppliers.
  • Family - We are a family-owned and operated business that values the importance of spending time with loved ones over a great meal.
